While I was checking my facebook, I found someone had posted a link to Rainbow Transsisters. Don’t you dare think I’m done with you. I’m gonna keep blogging. Like my own personal diary. Let you guys know important stuff. When I leave for Arizona, I do not know if I will be a Paid apprentice so I will still rely on you guys for quite some time.

what will I be an apprentice of? Someone who works on stones and crystals, and to my belief, sometimes jewelry. I will be learning to polish stones, cut and shape crystals and stones, and possibly be making crystals. I believe I’m in for a fun time in Arizona!

That’s all for today, my beloved followers. Thank you for your time! If I may ask only a minute more please be a patron! Just send it to my paypal email above. You don’t have to donate much. Who am I?

My preferred name is Cynthia. I like video games, anime, music books, and yet I still have a love of nature. Sometimes when I feel too stressed because of modern life. I just go climb a tree and lay in it for hours on end. But that doesn’t work wonders and make it all go away. Some thoughts still pierce through the tranquility of nature. These thoughts are caused by my Dysphoria*. Being transgender in the house I live in is horrible. I was talking to a friend online who went through almost the same thing. Being in the closet of gayity for him. But what about me? What makes me similar to him? I am in the closet about being pansexual and homoromantic and being transgender. I am 19 years old and I was never taught to drive. Uber? That’s not an option because I live in the middle of no where. So I can’t escape my home if the worst to happen would happen if I came out because my parents are Homophobic as well as Transphobic. Being both, my only option is to get the hell out of here. But how can I do that without a car or a job? That’s where you, my beloved Patreon, come in. I need your help if I am to ever get out of this place. I will tell you what my life is like here and eventually this. Blog will evolve into a Vlog when I get to freedom. It may be a while before that starts but I’ll keep you updated on how I’m getting settled in my new place.

*Dysphoria is the extreme hate or dislike of one’s body. It is common in transgenders.
